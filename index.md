I am Mainak, a graduate from [Maulana Abul Kalam Azad Institute of Technology (MAKAUT), West Bengal](https://makautwb.ac.in/) from where I have completed my B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineeering. Most recently, I was as a Junior Project Engineer and Project Assistant at the prestigious [Complex Network Research Group (CNeRG) Lab](https://cnerg-iitkgp.github.io/), Department of Computer Science Engineering, [IIT Kharagpur](https://www.iitkgp.ac.in/) where I worked on applying various Language Models to downstream several Natural Language Processing (NLP) tasks such as cross-lingual summarization, machine translation and question-answering, with a specific focus on the legal domain under [Prof. Saptarshi Ghosh](https://sites.google.com/site/saptarshighosh/). Broadly, I am interested in making Language Models safe, secured and aligned to user's needs. I am also interested in robustness and analysis.

Before that, I was a Research Intern at the Futuristic Wireless and Network Technologies Group, G.S. Sanyal School of Telecommunications, [IIT Kharagpur](https://www.iitkgp.ac.in/), where I worked with [Prof. Debarati Sen](https://sites.google.com/view/debarati-sen) on designing modern digital communication systems by fine-tuning Deep Neural Networks.

**Keywords:** Language Models, Cross-lingual Summarization, Alignment, Deep Neural Networks

In the past, I was fortunate to intern at the [IIT (ISM) Dhanbad](https://www.iitism.ac.in/) under [Prof. Samrat Mukhopadhyay](https://sites.google.com/view/samratspace/home?authuser=0), focusing on implementation and testing on Generalized Online Caching Algorithms. I also has an amazing time at [Suvidha Foundation](https://suvidhafoundationedutech.org/) where I developed a flower classification model using CNNs and ML Classification Models to classify 56 different types of flowers.
